HVAC Maintenance

Keep your heating and cooling systems running at peak efficiency with our professional HVAC maintenance plans. Heating & AC Repair

When your air conditioning stops cooling or your furnace won't ignite, Razo Heating & Air Conditioning delivers fast, reliable HVAC repair services throughout Gainesville and surrounding areas. Our goal is same-day resolution so you can get back to comfortable living without the stress of prolonged downtime.

Indoor Air Quality

Breathe easier with our indoor air quality services designed for Georgia's humid climate. We install and maintain air purifiers, UV germicidal lights, whole-home dehumidifiers, and advanced air filtration systems that remove allergens, mold spores, dust, and pollutants. Improve the health and comfort of your family or employees with cleaner, fresher indoor air — an essential upgrade for allergy sufferers and anyone concerned about air quality.

Duct Cleaning & Sealing

Leaky or dirty ductwork can waste up to 30% of your heated or cooled air. Razo Heating & Air Conditioning's professional duct cleaning and sealing services improve airflow, enhance indoor air quality, and reduce energy consumption in your Gainesville-area home or commercial building. Our technicians use state-of-the-art equipment to remove built-up dust, debris, and contaminants while sealing gaps to maximize your HVAC system's efficiency.

Who We Are

About Razo Heating & Air Conditioning

Razo Heating & Air Conditioning is a family-owned and operated heating and air conditioning company based right here in Gainesville, Georgia. Owner Miguel Razo and his family grew up in Gainesville and continue to call it home — which means when we service your HVAC system, we're taking care of our own neighbors.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience in the HVAC industry, our team has completed more than 1,000 residential and commercial jobs across North East Georgia. From emergency air conditioning repairs in the middle of a July heatwave to full heating system replacements before winter sets in, we've built our reputation on honest pricing, quality workmanship, and genuine care for every customer.

We are a fully licensed and insured HVAC contractor specializing in energy-efficient heating and cooling solutions for homes and businesses in Gainesville, Flowery Branch, Buford, Suwanee, Cumming, Alpharetta, Roswell, and surrounding communities. Whether you need a routine furnace tune-up, a ductless mini-split installation, or a complete commercial HVAC overhaul, Razo Heating & Air Conditioning delivers reliable comfort you can count on — backed by a team that truly knows North Georgia's climate.

We treat every home and business like it's our own. That's the Razo family promise.
